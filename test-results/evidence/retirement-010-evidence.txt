Features Implemented:
---------------------

1. YEAR-BY-YEAR PROJECTION
   - RunProjection() / RunProjectionWithConfig() methods
   - Tracks all account balances through accumulation and retirement phases
   - Calculates Social Security and pension income
   - Handles inflation adjustments
   - Returns detailed YearProjection for each year

2. WITHDRAWAL STRATEGY MODELING
   - Five strategies: ProRata, TaxableFirst, TraditionalFirst, RothFirst, TaxOptimized
   - CalculateWithdrawal() method with tax-aware calculations
   - Supports multiple account types: Taxable, Traditional, Roth, HSA
   - Handles tax grossing-up for traditional account withdrawals

3. ROTH CONVERSION LADDER
   - CalculateRothConversionLadder() method
   - Respects 5-year seasoning rule for penalty-free access
   - Optimizes conversions to fill lower tax brackets
   - calculateOptimalConversion() finds best conversion amount
   - Uses 2024 tax brackets for planning

4. SEPP/72(t) CALCULATIONS
   - CalculateSEPP() method with three IRS-approved methods:
     * RequiredMinimumDistribution
     * FixedAmortization
     * FixedAnnuitization
   - CompareSEPPMethods() for comparing all methods
   - Uses IRS Single Life Expectancy Table
   - Proper amortization/annuitization formulas
